Research Profile

Survey methodology, machine learning, and computational social science.

My research focuses on how data is generated, measured, modeled, and interpreted in complex social systems. I work across survey methodology, NLP, LLM-assisted annotation, public discourse, and data-centric machine learning, with an emphasis on measurement error, bias, uncertainty, sampling, and inference from human-generated data.
