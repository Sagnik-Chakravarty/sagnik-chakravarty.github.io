## Summary
Developed graph-learning and optimization methods for personalized travel recommendation.

## Objective
Build a personalized route recommendation framework that accounts for preferences, traffic, and travel constraints.

## Abstract
The thesis combined graph neural networks, OSMnx/NetworkX road graphs, and Particle Swarm Optimization for route recommendation in Goa.

## Motivation
Traditional routing tools optimize distance or time, but often ignore personalized tourist preferences and contextual travel behavior.

## Methods
OSMnx, NetworkX, GNNs, self-attention models, Particle Swarm Optimization, web-scraped travel data, and route scoring.

## Results
The system achieved 88% Precision@10 and 83% Recall@10 for personalized route recommendation.

## Insight
Demonstrates end-to-end applied ML engineering: graph construction, neural ranking, and optimization.
