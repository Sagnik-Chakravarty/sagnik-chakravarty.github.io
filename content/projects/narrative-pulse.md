## Summary
Interactive system for analyzing public sentiment and narrative patterns in legal cases using multi-platform discourse data.

## Objective
Build a live legal narrative research system that measures public discourse, sentiment, engagement, and event-aligned narrative shifts.

## Abstract
Narrative Pulse combines social and news data pipelines, sentiment analysis, topic modeling, temporal alignment, and dashboarding to support legal narrative intelligence.

## Motivation
High-profile legal cases unfold in a public information environment where social platforms, news coverage, and influencer activity can shift public interpretation over time.

## Methods
Multi-platform scraping, text cleaning, sentiment analysis, topic modeling, event-window comparison, engagement aggregation, and Streamlit dashboard development.

## Results
Delivered a live dashboard and report for querying, visualizing, and analyzing discourse patterns surrounding the YSL RICO Trial.

## Insight
Public sentiment shifts can be measured as temporal signals connected to events, influencer narratives, and platform-specific discourse behavior.
