## Summary
Used factorial design to evaluate CNN architecture and hyperparameter behavior.

## Objective
Use experimental design to systematically compare computer-vision model settings.

## Abstract
The project tested CNN-style architectures and hyperparameter combinations using factorial design logic on CIFAR-10.

## Motivation
Deep-learning performance depends heavily on architecture and hyperparameter choices, which require systematic evaluation.

## Methods
CNNs, VGG/ResNet-style comparisons, CIFAR-10, hyperparameter combinations, factorial design.

## Results
Showed how factorial design can make model tuning more interpretable and structured.

## Insight
Combines experimental design with computer-vision model evaluation.
