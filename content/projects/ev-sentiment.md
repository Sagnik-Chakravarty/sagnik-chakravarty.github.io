## Summary
Compared public EV sentiment across Reddit, The New York Times, and LLM-generated responses.

## Objective
Evaluate whether LLM-generated responses approximate public and media sentiment about electric vehicles.

## Abstract
The project collected Reddit and New York Times EV discourse, applied sentiment modeling, and compared observed sentiment to LLM-generated persona outputs.

## Motivation
LLMs are sometimes treated as proxies for public opinion, but their outputs may diverge from actual social/media discourse.

## Methods
Reddit and NYT collection, DistilBERT sentiment analysis, LLM persona prompting, ANOVA, effect-size comparison.

## Results
NYT and Reddit sentiment skewed negative, while LLM responses were much more positive.

## Insight
LLMs should not be used as public-opinion proxies without validation against real human-generated discourse.
