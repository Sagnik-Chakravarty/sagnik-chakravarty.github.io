## Summary
Compared PageRank, HITS, and random-walk ranking behavior on citation networks.

## Objective
Evaluate how graph-ranking algorithms differ in identifying influence and authority.

## Abstract
The project implemented and compared ranking algorithms on synthetic and real citation-network data.

## Motivation
Network ranking depends strongly on how algorithms define importance, authority, and hub structure.

## Methods
PageRank, HITS, random walk, citation-network construction, Spearman rank correlation.

## Results
Compared algorithmic behavior and ranking stability across network settings.

## Insight
Shows graph-algorithm knowledge and evaluation of ranking outputs.
