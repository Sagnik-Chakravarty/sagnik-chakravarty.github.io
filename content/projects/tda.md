## Summary
Applied topological data analysis to biomolecular structure representation; this work won the Best Paper Award at NCSET 2024.

## Objective
Use topology to identify global structural patterns in DNA/RNA data.

## Abstract
The project used Mapper and persistent homology to study structural patterns, loops, voids, and irregularities in biomolecular data.

## Motivation
Sequence-level analysis can miss global structural information in biomolecules.

## Methods
Topological Data Analysis, Mapper, persistent homology, structural feature interpretation.

## Results
Demonstrated how topology can support molecular structure analysis by bridging local and global patterns, and received the Best Paper Award at NCSET 2024.

## Insight
Shows mathematical breadth and early research training in nonstandard data representation.
