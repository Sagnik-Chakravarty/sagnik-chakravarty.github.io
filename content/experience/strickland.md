Lead data analyst for high-stakes legal narrative research analyzing discourse surrounding the YSL RICO Trial.

Built Playwright web scraping pipelines to collect multi-platform discourse from Reddit, X/Twitter, Instagram, Facebook, and news sources.

Used sentiment analysis, topic modeling, temporal pattern extraction, and DAG-based causal reasoning to measure sentiment shifts linked to key events and influencer-driven narratives.

Delivered Narrative Pulse (https://narrativepulse.streamlit.app/) as a live Streamlit dashboard for querying, visualizing, and analyzing discourse in real time.
