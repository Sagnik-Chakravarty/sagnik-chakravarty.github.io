
Title: FrameScope: A Multimodal Pipeline for Analyzing AI Discourse through Metaphors and Stance
Motivation:
Public understanding of AI is shaped by narratives, metaphors, and framing rather than raw facts.
Methods:
- LLM-assisted metaphor detection (19 categories)
- Stance classification (favor, against, neutral)
- Multimodal data pipeline (news + Reddit)
Results:
- Dominant metaphors: helper_tool (~23.6%), weapon_threat (~17.8%), human_replacement (~13.9%)
- Temporal increase in threat-related metaphors post-2022
Tools:
Python, LLM APIs, SQLite, Streamlit
Conference:
AAPOR
